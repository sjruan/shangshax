>>>THE CITY OF SZYMBORSKA

As an experimental project, the City of Szymborska tests the compatibility of World-building and Poetry.An imaginary city were built up in an exhibition space, where you can feel free to explore and experience different ways of looking at a poetry, hopefully, you'll find a new perspective to look at the world.11 spots are displayed as individual units, you're welcome to touch, listen and interact with.
